Taylor Bertman is a Sophomore in Design and Technology (Game Design Tract) at Parsons School of Design based in New York City.

Taylor has been immersed in the game world since before she can remember, and is thrilled to finally be able to study it and apply it in her daily life. She knows multiple coding languages such as Python, p5.js, and CSharp, and continuously educates herself on modern and popular games in the game world to always remain up to date. Her favorite game genres are indie games farm sims and narratives, as well as life sims. Some of her current favorite games are The Sims 4, Marvel Rivals, Fields of Mistria, Stardew Valley, and Hollow Night! As a game designer, Taylor thrives to design games that instigate meaning and call for change.

Outside of School, Taylor also involves herself in many beneificial extracurriculars!

Taylor also dabbles in many fields that keep heer mind thinking and keep her sould learning. Part of the time, she works as a Content Creator for The New School Welcome Center where she aspires to make posts about the authentic student experience and be a source of communication for other prospective students. She also works as a Stage Manager in the Theatrical world, earning credits Off-Broadway and Off-Off Broadway, aspiring to one day make her Stage Managerial Broadway Debut! She also spends her time as the Vice-President of the Games Club and an Officer of the Tabletop Games Club, where the goal is to bring people together to a space curated for gamers to meet other gamers and interact with one another..
